Fake Speech


Objective

Create a video where someone words are taken of context and make them say something different than what they originally were.

Requirements

1. The video should be at least 45 seconds long.

2. Provide a script of what the person is supposed to be saying.

3. Subissions should also include a short description of the techniques and tools you used to create the video.
